# **App Name**: Meddlington Reader

## Core Features:

- Homepage Display: Showcase the brand's identity with a hero section featuring the 'spectrum illustration', latest news, and highlighted books.
- Book Catalog: Browse a comprehensive list of all published books and upcoming titles ('coming soon'), complete with cover images, titles, and authors, with filtering capabilities. Each book listing will include a 'Buy it Now' button linking to Amazon for individual purchases, or a 'Coming Soon' indicator.
- Individual Book Detail Pages: Dedicated pages for each book providing extended descriptions, author biographies, genre information, and a 'Buy it Now' button linking to Amazon for individual purchases. Upcoming titles will display a 'Coming Soon' message instead of purchase options.
- Wholesale & Contact Inquiry Forms: Provide a page with information for wholesale partners and general inquiries, each featuring a user-friendly form for submission. On the wholesale page, book listings will include a 'Stock this title' button linking to Ingram Spark for wholesale orders, with 'Coming Soon' indicators for unreleased titles. The general contact form will use https://formspree.io/f/mjgeqwnw.
- AI-Assisted Book Blurb Generator: A tool that leverages generative AI to create compelling and varied marketing blurbs for books, based on provided summaries or keywords, to assist publishers.
- Global Navigation: Implement a clear and intuitive navigation menu prominently placed on the right, linking to Home, Books, Wholesale, and Contact pages.
- Clickable Logo: The brand logo, positioned top-left, will be present on all pages and clickable, allowing users to return to the homepage from any section of the site.
- Homepage Engaging Image: Display an engaging image, such as a child reading a book, lower down on the homepage after the hero section to break up formal text and add visual interest.

## Style Guidelines:

- A light color scheme centered around warm, earthy tones. The background will be a subtle sandy beige (#ECE6D5), evoking comfort and a literary feel. The primary color, used for key headings and elements, will be a rich, sophisticated terracotta-brown (#995A29), providing a professional contrast. An accent of muted brick-red (#AC474B) will highlight interactive elements and calls to action, maintaining a warm and inviting palette.
- For a blend of professional yet playful style, 'Poppins' (sans-serif) will be used for headlines, providing a modern and geometric touch. 'Alegreya' (serif) will be used for body text, offering an elegant, intellectual, and highly readable experience, ideal for literary content.
- Utilize icons that are friendly and subtly illustrative, aligning with literary themes like books, open pages, or artistic elements, while maintaining a clean and modern aesthetic suitable for a professional publishing brand.
- A clean, asymmetrical layout with the brand logo placed top-left and primary navigation aligned to the right. The homepage will feature a prominent hero image with the 'spectrum illustration'. Ample white space will ensure readability and highlight content, maintaining a professional and contemporary publishing style across responsive designs.
- Subtle and sophisticated micro-animations for page transitions and hover states on interactive elements like book covers or navigation links. These animations will enhance user experience without detracting from the professional and focused content presentation.
